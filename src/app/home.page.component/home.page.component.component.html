Have you heard of ‘ECDYSIS’? No, it’s not the name of some strange medicine. Ecdysis, aka moulting, is the process where a snake sheds its skin. But why does a snake shed its skin? And why is this even being mentioned here? Let’s get into it:


1. Like the skin of a snake becomes inadequate as the snake grows, similarly your building does not accommodate the FSI, amenities and facilities in the market as they grow.

2. The old skin of the snake is a home for pests which are not good for the health of the snake. Just like that, age and wear-and-tear are not good for the health of the building.

3. So, when it’s time, a snake sheds its skin to grow and live a long life. And for societies, this process of renewal and rejuvenation for a long life is redevelopment!

One final science fact: before the snake sheds its skin, it secretes a special fluid between the old and the new skin. The fluid ensures a smooth process of shedding skin. And that’s the role we play in the redevelopment of your society! We are “CASTLERS” .
