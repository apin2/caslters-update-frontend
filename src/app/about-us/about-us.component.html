What Differentiate Us

With aim to provide solution to every difficulty and obstacle in Redevelopment we are providing end to end consultancy in orthodox method of development, Self-development and specially designed mode of development which we name as “Limited Self-Development”.
“Conventional development” is an orthodox mode of re-development where society appoints suitable developer to reach to their goal of redevelopment. Though it is well known form of redevelopment but there are many obstacles in this form of development and most prominent one is Transparency. It is common practice that society selects developer by comparing offers given by some developers. In order to overcome this issue firstly we structure the deal for society by adopting spectrum analysis method and thereafter we proceed with completely online tendering process to select developers. We have totally curtailed human interference and influence in tendering process which enables society to select developer with complete impartiality and transparency.
Self-development is mode of development where society themselves contribute to develop their property with the help of expert agencies. It is not hidden fact that self-development gives better returns to the members of the society and more than that in some cases it is not feasible for developers to re- develop some societies and then Self-development becomes necessity for such society, but despite of that, this method has not held the strong roots in the market.

Out of many, one of the prominent reasons behind this is, listless approach of bank to finance housing societies. It is also seen that in most of the housing societies members are not physically as well as mentally prepared to take risk of taking task of re-development on their shoulders. Considering many of such obstacles we have come-up with unique solution to raise funds partly through bank and party through self-contribution which limits the financial burden of society members. We have also engaged expert architects, civil engineers, contractors with us who avail their support to the society for self-development.
Limited self-development is mode of development where society develop their property by appointing developer, with assured sale to the developers, this uniquely designed mode of re-development has characteristics of conventional as well as self-development. With our specialized method of Spectrum Analysis we structure the deal in such manner that society could be developed with minimum investment on the part of society members. This allows us to serve societies to secure development through mode of Limited self-development.
Certainly there are various difficulties in the re-development which some time render societies in hopeless situation of non-possibility of re-development, but we assure you to take you to your destiny of redevelopment in either of three of modes of re-development.

Our Characteristic Features

Methodological approach: With our expertise and also with support of our knowledge partners, we have taken deep insight of entire re-development process and thereby we have developed Standard operating procedure which ensures every minute compliance to complete the redevelopment in timely manner.
Know your potential at first meet: At the very first presentation we provide tentative feasibility of project which includes financial viability of project, and technical feasibility. It also includes structure of the deal with tentative increased area statement of each tenement and statement of other benefits such as rent, shifting charges, corpus fund, and betterment funds.
Know project updates without gathering: We understand that it is right of every member to know progress of the redevelopment project however it is impractical to gather every time for getting updates. No worries! Team castlers is here to support you, we keep society members updated by online mechanism, each report we take and progress we make gets updated over dedicated web-page maintained for society and all its members.
Firewalls: The process of redevelopment is quite complicated as it involves moving entire families from owned houses to rented houses and back to the new owned houses. This entire task is under a shadow of risk of project failure, which is the most prominent reason why societies and their members are hesitant towards the redevelopment of their properties. By causing “root cause analysis” of various risk involved in redevelopment process we have developed firewall mechanism which ensures hassle-free redevelopment of society.
Apple to Apple Comparison: At early stage we cause comparative analysis of existing flats on three parameters i.e. physical carpet, Sanctioned Carpet and RERA carpet, and in consideration of this, we conclude deal with developers on the basis of RERA carpet of existing flat to curtail carpet discrepancies between old flat and new flat.
Technical feasibility: Prior to appointment of developer we ascertain every minute technical aspect relating to re- development of the society to avoid project jams on technical grounds after appointment of developer.
Increased area feasibility: We ascertain increased area feasibility; to appreciate whether increased tenements can be accommodated in new building or not; we also resolve discrepancies if any regarding increased area which brings utmost clarity on successful implementation of redevelopment scheme.

Deal Structuring: We understand that success and satisfaction are two different aspects and “deal structuring” plays vital role in reaching goal of successful re-development with true satisfaction. However as on today this crucial aspect is limited to eye on increased area, which in turn lead it towards rigid approach, to resolve this issue we have come up with uniquely designed “SPECTRUM ANALYSIS METHOD” which allows us to provide tailor-made offers to the society which suits best to their needs. In spectrum analysis we consider seven facets namely 1) Road Width, 2) Market Price, 3) Purchase Appetite of Society, 4) Society’s contribution, 5) Other benefits, 6) Project Cost 7) Net profit ratio and by adjusting band width of each facet we formulate best suitable offer for society to gain true satisfaction from successful re-development.
Transparent and impartial tendering process: Transparency is our core value and for its practical implementation, we help society in selecting developer through tendering process. We understand that human interference always influence selection so with the help of our dynamic website we process tenders of society through completely web-driven tendering process.
Financial scrutiny: We believe financial soundness of developer is key factor in redevelopment process so while selecting developers for society we guide society to secure their interest by optimizing control over initial investment required for redevelopment project.
Standardized legal drafts: understanding threats and needs of redevelopment; and also to harmonize contractual obligations between society and developer, we have standardized several legal drafts such as Memorandum of understanding Development Agreement, Agreement of Permanent alternate accommodation etc.
Allotment of tenement: we ensure to keep control of society in the allotment of new flats to its members without intervention of developer.
Reinstating management & accounts: We have designed significant method to take over management of new building as well as accounts from the developer to avoid disputes amongst society and developer.
